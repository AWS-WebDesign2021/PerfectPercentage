Ideal App / Website for Globalization / Cultural Diversity



> An ideal application for globalization or cultural diversity would be that , it is very stictly regulated and dedicated to positive thoughts and discussions.

> It should have a group discussion feature , as well as a private messaging feature, but strictly regulated , if users post hate speech , they are banned

> Users can post medias but there is no like feature , comments are all visible to the user who posted before they are shown under their posts

> Good Vibes only

> There will be different groups and language tags created first for users to choose but they can also choose not to

> Strict ID and age verification system , one person can only create one account so if you're banned , you don't get to just create another account and continue roaming

> Application is for the good and improvement of the world only , no business promotion / advertisements

> Shares information about different activism activities that are accessible to users
