'Reflection'

> The making of this website gave me the chance to learn more with keyframes and look into hover functionalities.

> One of the difficulties I faced when coding this website was that the background animation for the header was very confusing. The sizing kept on going off the grid and it was very much frustrating.

> However , I was able to get it to work after I used overflow - hidden , so that everything stays within the header sizing.

> My favorite thing about this website is the transition that appears when hovered over , for example the text boxes (divs) , it was also fairly simple to code.

> Something to improve would be : make the background animation smoother, it seems a bit glitching when switching between the background images due to sizing problems..
