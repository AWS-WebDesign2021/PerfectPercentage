'Brief Intro About Website'

> This page was A Travel Guide to Taiwan - Spring Season

> Basic Info / Sceneries / Food / Traveling / COVID situation in Taiwan was introduced.

> It was a multiple page website , my first big project this school year.

> I took on a more different design between home and branch pages , as well as the header and nav bar.

> The style I used in this website I was able to incorporate a layout feeling with setting div width to 50% and 50% of the page.

'Website Display'


'Reflection'

> I really enjoyed making this site because it was about my home country , so I was extra excited to introduce it to people.

> I reviewed my previous code for the navigation bar and understood how each element played their roles, from then I was able to even further custom the navigation bar to my design.

> Playing with display percentages , having a design layout became easier without having to continually guess the pixels.

> I also had the chance to test out the overflow : scroll function under the pages and it was fascinating.

> One thing I would've changed about this website would be to better design the feedback page.
