'Course / General Reflection'

I really enjoyed taking this class because I find web design extremely fun and rewarding. This is my second year / time taking Mr.Considine's web design class, the first one allowed to me find a new passion and this one gave me the oppurtunity to dive deeper and to create way more elaborated projects. Web design class also gives me a lot of space for my own creativity and freedom to explore and learn new things I can do within this field. To be honest , web design isn't always the most satisfying. Things often wouldn't work out , the CSS doesn't come out the way I want it to , I'm missing a closing tag on an element , or the JavaScript simply does NOT make sense. However , it is always so rewarding and adrenaline pumping when I finally figure out something and it's working. I'd rank it 100/10 , one of my favorite feelings in the ENTIRE world. The projects I've chosen to showcase on my 2021 portfolio are all some of my proudest projects. Again , huge shoutout to Mr.Considine for being so laid back , challenging me to go beyond my limits but also stepping in and providing crucial support at the best timings. Web Design is definitely something I would continue learning and exploring in my future.
