BUZZFEED QUIZ

'Brief Intro About Website'

> This page was about DESIGNING A BUZZFEED QUIZ!!

> Hands down , 10/10 , definitely my favorite project of the year.

> The requirements for this website was to design a working buzzfeed quiz that gives users different results based on their answers / selections.

> Designing this website also made me realize how IMPORTANT comments are. For once I can look at my code a long time later and still be able to conprehend what each section stood for.

> Main Focus of this Project : Forms and JavaScript

> Special Requirement for this website : advertisement

'Website Display'


'Reflection'

> To start off , this website was extremely frustrating to make.

> JavaScript is very useful and interesting , but also is just a total pain to deal with.

> BUT !! I was introduced to the concept of parameters and I am OBSSESSED with the new world it led me to. It made coding the JavaScript so much more convenient.

> I did meet a roadblock when trying to calculate the total points for the user to land in different results , it was quickly fixed when Mr.Considine pointed out that I should've been using == for equal to rather than = ..-_- (but hey it was solved)

> I really really liked making this website because after solving Javascript problems the navigation was actually so smooth and just rewarding. I also tried out designing buttons and styling them through CSS , which made them much more visually satisfying.

> Also - the making of the advertisement was super fun , I tried out a low budget style of ad banner design that you often see on sketchy websites and it was amazing.
