facetiming my friend goes wrong

Just like every other night,after I return to my room, I facetime my friends. I called the first time and nobody picked up. I tried calling the second time, still nobody answered. Still not giving up, I called the third time. This time somebody picked up. Instead of my friend's voice, all I heard was screaming and a deep voice saying, "If you don't stop, the next is you." Opening my eyes, I took a deep breath. It's already the morning. My phone is in my hand. I had once more fallen asleep while facetiming my friend in another time zone.
