Northern Taiwan



> Northern Taiwan is the upper terminus of the soaring central mountains that bisect the long, narrow island.

> Highlights here range from the country's capital, Taipei, to the peaks and trails of Yangmingshan National Park.

> Close to the capital can be found a surprising mix of attractions, from displays of aboriginal culture and fascinating cave temples to a ceramics town filled with kilns and shops.




Northern Taiwan : Yangmingshan



> Yangmingshan National Park is often referred to as metropolitan Taipei’s "backyard garden," a place of endless natural adventure that offers a network of hiking trails. Especially beautiful scenaries in the spring.

> The north coast is characterized by bathing beaches, unusual rock formations, and the occasional temple and museum.

> Yangmingshan is far more than just a pleasant park with landscaped walks sitting at the summits of Taipei’s most famous nearby peaks, and it’s worth making the time to visit it.
