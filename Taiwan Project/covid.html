Taiwan : COVID



> Taiwan has been praised for its highly effective Covid-19 response.

> Taiwan, which has nearly 24 million citizens, has had only less than 1K cases and few deaths.

> Taiwan had a plan in place for years, which involved quarantines, contact tracing and wide availability of masks, among other things. read this article to learn more!





Travel to Taiwan



> Marked by the US CDC , Taiwan is currently at Level 1: COVID-19 Low. (Updated 03/22/2021)

> However , Taiwan has borders shut down currently for foreign travelers , not open to tourism.

> For now , Taiwan's borders are only open to people who are arriving for work , a Taiwanese citizen returning, or special cases approved by the governement. A 14+7 day government tracked quarantine is required for every traveler's arrival upon Taiwan.
