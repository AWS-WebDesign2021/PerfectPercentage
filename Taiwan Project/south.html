Southern Taiwan



> Southern Taiwan is a land of timeless rituals and vibrant folk culture.

> Expect a feast of original street snacks, flamboyant temples and enduring relics at every turn.

> Southern traditions are given a charismatic 21st-century spin as art greets industry by the harbour and chefs reinvent century-old dishes.




Southern Taiwan : Kenting National Park



> Kenting National Park (墾丁國家公園) drew lots of attention first as a setting for Taiwanese director Wei Te-sheng’s blockbuster Cape No. 7, and later for Ang Lee’s Life of Pi in 2012.

> The park, which occupies the entire southern tip of Taiwan, was drawing in flocks of visitors who came to swim, surf, snorkel, dive, hike and enjoy a little nightlife.

> The average Spring temperature is 21-25°C and it's usually warm enough for you to swim.
