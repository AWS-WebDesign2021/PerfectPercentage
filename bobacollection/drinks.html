Different Flavors and Introductions :

Classic Milk Tea



The Most Basic Flavor , usually just black tea and milk and tapioca, has a lighter color








Thai Milk Tea



Tastes like Classic Milk Tea Boba but 10x sweeter , made with black tea , condensed milk, milk and tapioca, usually orange






Matcha Milk Tea



Has a havier matcha taste to it ,made with either green tea / matcha powder , black tea, milk and tapioca, usually green






Taro Milk Tea



Described as sweet and nutty ,made with taro powder (food coloring) , black tea, milk and tapioca, usually purple







Brown Sugar Boba



Super sweet and usually comes a little bit warm , this one does not have tea in it , just boba cooked with brown sugar and milk, personal fav!




Passion Fruit Tea and Boba



Boba is more than just milk tea ! Often times also served with different flavored tea (usually fruity) and tapiocas!
