Overpopulation

> The UN expects the world population to exceed 8.5 billion by 2030, forcing us to considerably reduce the amount of waste
we generate through prevention, reduction, reuse and recycling

> Overpopulation is an undesirable condition where the number of the existing human population exceeds the actual carrying capacity of Earth.

> Other factors that leads to Overpopulation
  • reduced mortality rate
  • better medical facilities
  • an increase in immigration
  • lack of family planning and contraceptives use

Negative Effects that Overpopulation brings

> Depletion of Natural Resources
  • The Earth can only produce a limited amount of water and food, which is falling short of the current needs.

  • Enviromental Damage : cutting down forests, hunting wildlife in a reckless manner, causing pollution.
> Rise in Unemployment
  • When a country becomes overpopulated, it gives rise to unemployment as there are fewer jobs to support a large number of people.
> Malnutrition, Starvation and Famine
  • When the availability of resources is scarce, starvation, malnutrition, along with ill health and diseases caused by diet-deficiency become more likely.

Potential Solutions

> Better Education & Social Awareness
  • Educating the masses helps them understand the need to have one or two children at the most.
  • Imparting sex education to young kids at the elementary level should be a must.
  • Raise awareness among people regarding family planning and letting them know about serious after-effects of overpopulation







Where it Occurs

> Overpopulation can be observed all around the world, however, it is especially severe in some countries, especially observed in male-dominated societies.
  • sub-Saharan Africa
  • South and West Asia



> Overpopulation is more likely to be seen in these countries as the society is more traditional, not allowing abortions and/or has less family planning...general knowledge or access to contraceptives etc.





What can WE do to help?

> Use your social platforms to raise social awareness about Overpopulation!

> Educate those around you : tell people about it!

> Do family planning : use contraceptives if not wanting kids !
