What Internet Services Do Verizon Provide?


Below are different kinds of services Verizon provides :
  • 5G Home Internet
  • Fios (100% Fiber Optic Network)
  • High Speed Internet (DSL)

What Are the Differences?

> 5G Home Internet : Up to 940 Mbps download speed, 50Mbps upload speed, Wireless

> Fios : Up to 940 Mbps download speed, 880 Mbps upload speed, Wired + Wifi compatible

> High Speed Internet : Up to 15 Mbps download speed, 1 Mbps upload speed, Wired + Wifi compatible



What makes Verizon Standout as an ISP?

  • Unlike other ISPs, Verizon offers Fios, on a 100% fiber-optic network

    • - > It transmits data much more efficiently, because light signals transfer data much faster than traditional DSL or copper cable.

  • Fios works differently. It uses FTTH, or fiber to the home, which means the fiber cables are connected directly to your home
    instead of using fiber cables to carry data to a hub close to your home and then switch to coaxial cables or DSL for the last hop,
    like many other ISPs provide
